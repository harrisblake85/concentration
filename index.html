Instructions

Concentration is a memory game played by flipping over two cards (click a card to flip it) , if the two flipped cards share the same number/rank the cards are removed from the board. Remove all the cards from the table to win! Press the Play Button to start a new game! Press the ACE Button after starting a game to debug (only ACES remain on the board)
